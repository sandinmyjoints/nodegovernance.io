Govern Node Right.

Joyent recently announced that a new foundation will take control of Node.js. Important details about how the foundation will work have not yet been decided.

Now is the time to tell Joyent CEO Scott Hammond that as a Node user, you would like io.js's open technical governance model to form the basis for the Node.js foundation's technical committee.

io.js's open goverance model ensures a wide variety of developers and community members get a say in Node's evolution, allowing for faster adoption of new technologies such as ES6. If the Node.js foundation adopts it, the two projects can be merged into one again.
