Govern Node Right.

Joyent recently announced that a new foundation will take control of Node. Important details about how the foundation will work have not yet been decided.

Now is the time to tell Joyent CEO Scott Hammond that you'd like io.js's open technical governance model to form the basis for the Node foundation's technical committee.

io.js's open goverance model ensures Node's future is not beholden to the needs of one or a few large companies, but rather determined by technical leaders who represent a spectrum of Node uses and users. If the Node foundation adopts it, the two projects can be merged into one again.

Tweet @Scott_Hammond: I'm a @nodejs user. I'd like to see the #nodejs foundation adopt @official_iojs technical governance model.
